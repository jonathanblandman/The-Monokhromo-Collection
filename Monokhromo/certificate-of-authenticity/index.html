04 · b What the certificate guarantees

Origin

That the work is an original digital fine art photograph by Jonathan B. Landman, issued by The Monokhromo Collection.

Edition state

That the work is a numbered edition. The edition size will be stated on the certificate at time of issue.

Title

That you, as named on the certificate, are the recorded holder of the edition until you transfer it.

The certificate travels with the work.

If you acquire a print from a previous owner, the certificate is the formal proof of edition. Transfers are recorded by the studio on written request.
