Downcast Light

Her face turns away, and the image becomes an act of withholding. Downcast Light holds beauty in the space between visibility and retreat — a quiet study of profile, shadow, and inwardness.

Downcast Light is a black-and-white fine art portrait from Beauty — Studio Studies I by Jonathan B. Landman. The work uses profile, soft shadow, and restrained expression to create a quiet monochrome study. Released as a limited digital edition with Certificate of Authenticity and printing guidance.
