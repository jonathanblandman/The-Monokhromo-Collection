02 Collections

Collections in monochrome.

The work is organised into collections — each pursued slowly until the plates resolve. Below, the current two.

SERIES · 01 LIGHT · SILENCE 6 WORKS

Beauty

Beauty, in this practice, is what survives reduction.

A study in subjects asked to stand quietly. Portraits and forms held in monochrome until only their contour remains.

SERIES · 02 STONE · SHADOW FORTHCOMING

Malta in Shadow

Stone keeps a record of its weather, if you let it.

Limestone, walls, and weather on a small archipelago at the centre of the Mediterranean. A long study in shadow as architecture.
