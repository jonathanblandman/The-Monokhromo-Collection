00 · 3 From the house

A practice, not a feed. Each plate asks the room to stop moving.

The Monokhromo Collection issues monochrome fine art photographs as digital editions. After purchase, the master file is delivered to you, accompanied by a numbered certificate of authenticity and a short letter of printing guidance.

The work is yours to print, frame, and live with — at the size, on the paper, and at the moment that suits the wall. No open editions, no reissues, no compromise on the file itself.
